RETWEET FILTER

-> This filter lets you specify which Retweets you want to hide on a per-user basis.
-> Enter multiple users by separating their Twitter handles with commas (the Twitter handle is the unique identifier that appears after the @ sign in a profile).
-> Click the "SAVE" button and make sure "Hide Retweets" is enabled to see the changes.

EXAMPLE: If you enter "LZZYHALE, Microsoft", then only Retweets by @LZZYHALE and @Microsoft will get hidden. All other Retweets will NOT be hidden.
