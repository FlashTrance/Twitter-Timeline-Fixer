Note: If the extension appears not to work, please refresh your Twitter page and try again.

-> This extension allows you to hide the following types of Tweets from your Timeline: "Liked", "Reply", "Followed By", "Follow Topic", and "Retweet" (examples of each type of Tweet are shown below).
-> You can hide Retweets on a per-user basis by clicking on the "Setup Retweet Filter" button in the extension pop-up window.
-> Please note that this extension only hides Tweets; it does not hide other sections like the "Who to Follow" section. For this, the developer recommends "Twitter Tamer" by grantwinney.
